Activity selection game

Three possible activities can be selected based off two rounds of dice rolls.

The round table activity is where each team member provides an update to the rest of the team about their tasks. The show the thing activity is a chance for a team member to show their latest work to the team and get constructive feedback. The wild card activity is a chance for the team to choose how to spend the remainder of the meeting time together without a predefined selection.

See Table 1 for probabilities.
